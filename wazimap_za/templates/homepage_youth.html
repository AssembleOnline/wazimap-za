About {{ WAZIMAP.name }}

{{ WAZIMAP.name }} is a tool to help make make youth information more available in the Western Cape. It is a collaboration between City of Cape Town, UCT Poverity and Inequality Index, Western Cape Government, Stats SA and Code for South Africa.
